Welcome to our Minecraft world, Vaservah! We are three cheeky kiiiids who have been playing Minecraft together since 2017 and we decided to start a blog to share our adventures with the world.

Vaservah is filled with many wonders and challenges. We have built massive railways, buildings, explored vast chasms, and even defeated the Ender Dragon.

As we explore our world, we discover new things every day. We encounter new biomes, discover hidden treasures, and stumble upon unexpected surprises. We are always on the lookout for new adventures to undertake and new challenges to overcome.

We are now on a mission to defeat Herobrine and his minion Derek II, former mayor of Retardsville.

We hope to share our passion for Minecraft with others. We will be documenting our adventures, sharing tips and tricks, and showcasing some of our most impressive creations. We will also be providing insights into the game mechanics and discussing the latest updates to the game.

We invite you to join us on our journey through our Minecraft world. Whether you are an experienced player or just starting out, there is something for everyone here.

If you have any comments, questions, concerns please send us a message via the contact form.
